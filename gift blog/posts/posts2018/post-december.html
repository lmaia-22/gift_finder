Christmas is expensive

There's no best feeling than giving except feeling your pocktes full

Posted by Luís Maia on December 29, 2018

This is the perfect time to talk about presents and what type of presents you can buy with a limit budget because unfortnelly we need money to survive

Most people think that only money can buy the perfect gifts to someone but that's not true. Let me tell you a story of one of my gifts to a co-worker who's mother died in the holidays.

This story may give you chills

So the mother of a co-worker of mine named Elsa pasted away in the holidays so i decided to give a generous gift that reminded her of her mother. She always liked disney princesses so I though since her name is Elsa i made a bust of the Elsa of frozen with a support in the base where I put a photo of her mother. Of course this was made with the help of 3D printer and some designs I saw in the Thingverse.

In addition I did a little card that had a snow flake it the following message, "People say that time heals everything but that's not true. Time is our biggest enemy so I give you this Elsa so that can help you freeze the memories you had with her for all the eternity, doesn't matter if they are good or bad, you freeze it all cause someday you will want to remeber and you can't."

I putted the gift in her desk after she went home so that in the next day the first thing she will do is open the present. Needless to say that she went over my desk when found out it was from me and gave me a hug and started crying saying that she absolutely love it.

Photography was a way for me to freeze time and to capture the moments that were happy and healthy. I saw a photo as a way to go back to a memory if I ever needed to. By Rachel Morrison - Born: April 27, 1978
