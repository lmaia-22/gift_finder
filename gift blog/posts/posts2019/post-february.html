Imagination the best weapon

Use it at your own risk.

Posted by Luís Maia on February 13, 2019

In this month a friend of mine wish bithday was in this month also had a little bunny named "Fluffy the Bunny" who died of a heart attack. So I though in giving her a replacement. I didn't want to give her a really one that was to easy. So I began the search for a replacement that wasn't alive or dead.

Fluffy the Bunny

So i went in a store of home products, and I found the perfect little bunny, whose name I can't remeber, but anyaway the bunny was fluffy and was holding a very cute fluffy blanket. Needless to say, I bought it. And also wrote a card to her, as it was from the bunny to her.

So to write this card I though. If the bunny could speak what would it say? *Sniff Sniff* I wrote the following: "Hello Patricia, I hope everything is fine with you. Everything is okay with me, I'm in the rabbit heaven where there are plenty of carrots and space to run. Finally I met my idol "Bugs Bunny", he is funny and different from the other rabbits, but I think you would like him the same. I know you miss me a lot, I miss you too, but unfortunately I can't go back. So that you do not feel sad, I will order my cousin to take care of you and I leave with him a fluffy blanket, not as much as me but it helps make the longing a little less painful. In memory of man's best friend, who is not a dog, not a cat, but a rabbit, because any animal is our friend if we love him ENOUGH. Thank you for loving me, a sniffer full of love and affection from your friend. FluffytheBunny."

Ideas are like rabbits. You get a couple and learn how to handle them, and pretty soon you have a dozen. By John Steinbeck - Born: February 27, 1902 - Died: December 20, 1968
