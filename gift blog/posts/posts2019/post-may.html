May the odds

Giving a gift is not a game of odds.

Posted by Luís Maia on May 22, 2019

Well this month, I'm talking about a gift I gave to a friend of mine who works on the design department. The nickname of this girl was squirrel because... I think you now why, but nevertheless it became a cute nickname at work and she likes it. She had a rough path because of that nickname but now she owned it and I couldn't be happier.

The Skillas

The title is the name she gave to the gift I made and the gift was a squirtel with nuts. I'll explain I found a cute design of a squirrel in a very famous website of 3D designs named THINGVERSE

So I decide to do it in my 3D printer along with some nuts to create a cute environment for the squirrel. And since she is from design I did it in white and let her do the paint.

In addition I decided to do a little note for her and give her a kinder surprise, she loves those. The note said:

"We must not let the past define our future, we must accept and focus on the present, because those actions and decisions are the ones that will define us. Whoever we were five or 10 years ago, has nothing to do with the person we are today. You're one of the coolest, sweetest girls I know, and also bipolar, but no-one's bound to wake up in good mood every day. I hope you like the gift, because like him both are small, cute, intelligent and like to eat. Ps: it's for you to eat, not him "

She loved the present and it's no need to say that she devoured in the moment the kinder surprise.

A squirrel dying in front of your house may be more relevant to your interests right now than people dying in Africa. By Mark Zuckerberg - Born: May 14, 1984
