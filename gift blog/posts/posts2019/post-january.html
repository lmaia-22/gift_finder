Money doesnt buy the perfect gift

Although a Lambo can make me happy faster than it gets from 0 to 60

Posted by Luís Maia on January 23, 2019

A friend of mine from work every single she wears a black dress with the intense red in her lips and nails, well she got pregnant of a baby girl. So to congratulate her I decided to buy a gift.

So what did I do? This gift was one that was meant to be you know? So i went into a store named Claire's looking for something interraly differently when I stumbled across a cute little fluffy pink pig and I had the craziest ideia, so I bought it.

The pig and the lipstick

The pig didn't have any lips, so I found a design of ones in the Thingverse and I did them in the 3D printer with a small difference with a small hole trough them. After I painted them with live red and passed a string throw them and put the lips in the pig. You may thing I'm making fun of her by calling her pig but she exactly the opposite. I also bought the lipstick with the red she uses it. And put all in a box of protein I had home. I wrapp it and done.

Also I wrote a small card to give to her with the following message: "Of all the farm animals I think the pig is the animal that most resembles you, because even without red lipstick, it can be the most beautiful pig in the pigsty. I'm playing with you, because of course I'm the prettiest little pig here on the farm. To try to steal the throne I leave you a small gift, because I think that when you use we will be tied in the 1st place. With a lot of love and affection from your neighbor from downsatirs"
