About Me

This is what I do.

Simple man with simple ideas. It turns out that these ideas are always well received. What are these ideas? These ideas are ideas for giving gifts to people, family, friends, girlfriend, co-workers, whomever you want. Sometimes even my friends and family come and ask me for advice.

I am proud owner of a 3D printer wich helps a lot to design my own gifts and turn the ideia in reality, but before the printer I already had the "gift" for choosing gifts, pun not intended. Of course knowing the person, what she does, her passions, hobbies and how her brain works is half the work done. The other half is more difficult, so I created this blog to help you choose and get some ideias.

If you want to know more about the gifts I bought or have made for my friends and family check my posts, I have a new one every month with new gifts and the reasons. It the studies and work it's difficult to post every week. But if you have a story about a gift and want to share it or you want advice send me a message in the contact form, I will reply as soon as possible.
